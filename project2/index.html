Our second trip was to Rome. We took a train to Rome and spent the day visiting all the different tourist sites. We went to the vatican, the collussium, and finally The Trevi Fountain.

Once we got off the train we walked a blck or two and got to Spanish Steps. We stayed at a hotel at the top of the Steps and set out to tour Rome

The first place went to in Rome was Vatican City. My parents set up a private tour of the city and we spent about 3-4 hours viewing all of Vatican City. This Picture was taken from a porch like area connected to the Vatican.

The last place that went through was the Sistine Chapel. We weren't supposed to take pictures or anything but I was able to sneak in a quick video of the ceiling. Something that I found to be very interesting about the chapel is the detail in the ceiling as you go from the back of the chapel to the front. The pictures not only get bigger but have much less detail when oyu get to the front.

We went passed the Collusium and walked down a path hoping to catch a view of the ancient ruins but we were kicked out. My brother Thomas took this picture as we were walking away from the ruins.

once leaving Rome we set out to the town where Padre Pio's shrine is. It took us a while to get there so we had to stay over at a nearby town and visit it tomorrow. We were able to catch some pretty awesome pictures along the way and catch some nice sites as the sun set.

Our last trip that we took was to Venice. We took a day trip to Venice and saw a lot of beautiful sites whether walking or on a gondola.

The first thing we saw went we got to Venice was a church decorated in beautiful mosaic designs. Gold Mosaics cover the entire ceiling of the church.

After walking through the church we were able to go to the top of the church where we were led out onto a balcony. From the balcony you could see a piazza as well as giant walkway that led to the waterfront, like shown in the photograph.

The family split up and I went with my brother. We were able to cover a lot more ground then if we just went as an entire family. In this photographer we are posing wearing the fedoras we bought along the way. behind us is one of the most popular bridges in Venice, The Rialto Bridge.
